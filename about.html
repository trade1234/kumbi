Our Story

Kumbi Oil Trading PLC is among the key players in the fuel, oil, and lubricants supply industry in Ethiopia, applying innovative technologies to improve logistics and deliver the highest quality services to its clients. With operations that embrace many parts of Ethiopia, we strive to be the company of choice for clients, partners, customers, and employees. Kumbi Oil Trading PLC was established in Ethiopia on May 17, 2019, and registered under the Ethiopian Commercial Registration and Business License Proc No. 980/2016., as a private limited liability company, to pursue business in the Petroleum and Petroleum Products Supply Industry .
