Who We Are

Kumbi Oil Trading PLC, established on May 17, 2019, is a leading player in Ethiopia's fuel, oil, and lubricants supply industry. We leverage innovative technologies to enhance logistics and provide top-quality services across various regions of Ethiopia. As a private limited liability company, we have expanded our operations to include importing engine oils, fuel dispenser machines, vehicle spare parts, industrial chemicals, rubber and plastic materials, metal products, and more. Additionally, we export agricultural commodities such as oil crops and coffee.
